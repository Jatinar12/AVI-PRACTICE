Science & Technology
Science and technology are important parts of our day to day life.
We get up in the morning from the ringing of our alarm clocks and go to bed at night after switching our lights off.
All these luxuries that we are able to afford are a resultant of science and technology.
Most importantly, how we can do all this in a short time are because of the advancement of science and technology only.
It is hard to imagine our life now without science and technology. Indeed our existence itself depends on it now.
Every day new technologies are coming up which are making human life easier and more comfortable.
Thus, we live in an era of science and technology.
